# Global Sales Data Analysis using Microsoft Excel

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a large global sales dataset using Microsoft Excel to identify business trends, product performance, and revenue patterns. The goal of the analysis is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.

The dataset contains approximately **30,000 sales records** with detailed information about products, customers, pricing, discounts, and sales channels. Using Excel's analytical features, the data was cleaned, organized, and analyzed to evaluate sales performance across multiple dimensions such as product categories, regions, and time periods.

## Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following key fields:

* Order ID
* Customer ID
* Order Date
* Brand
* Model Name
* Product Category
* Gender
* Size
* Color
* Base Price (USD)
* Discount Percentage
* Final Price (USD)
* Units Sold
* Revenue (USD)
* Payment Method
* Sales Channel
* Country
* Customer Income Level
* Customer Rating
* Year
* Month
* Month Name

## Objectives of the Project

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing product categories and brands
* Evaluate revenue trends over time
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Study the impact of discounts on sales performance
* Compare sales performance across regions and countries

## Analysis Performed

### Sales Performance Analysis

Evaluated total revenue generated and total units sold to understand overall business performance.

### Product Performance Analysis

Analyzed product categories, brands, and models to identify top-selling products.

### Customer Behavior Analysis

Examined customer income levels, ratings, and purchasing trends.

### Discount Impact Analysis

Studied the relationship between discount percentages and final product prices to evaluate pricing strategies.

### Regional Sales Analysis

Analyzed sales distribution across different countries to identify strong markets.

### Time-Based Sales Analysis

Examined monthly and yearly sales trends to detect seasonal patterns.
